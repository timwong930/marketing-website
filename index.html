The deep clean before upkeep.

All plans begin with a one-time deep clean: claim and verify listings, fix wrong info, upload 10–15 good photos, clean up duplicates, and repair obvious website/mobile issues. After that, your chosen monthly tier begins.

FAQ

Questions a coffee shop owner actually asks.

No runaround, no jargon. Here's what café owners ask us most.

Why is there a setup fee on top of the monthly price?+

The setup fee covers the heavy lifting: claiming and cleaning your Google Business and Yelp profiles, verifying info, uploading initial photos, and fixing obvious website/mobile problems before monthly upkeep begins.

Do you rebuild my site from scratch?+

Not always. We start by fixing what hurts customers most: outdated hours, broken menus, slow mobile experience, and confusing listings. If a rebuild makes more sense, we'll explain why and when.

How do you work with multiple locations?+

Each location has its own hours, menu, photos, and reviews, so we treat each as its own micro-presence with a smaller add-on fee per location.

What do I actually see each month?+

You get a short, owner-friendly summary: what changed, how many visits and clicks you got, what reviews came in, and which fixes we made that month.
