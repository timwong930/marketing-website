# AI Context — Roast & Reach

This repository is a public landing page for **Roast & Reach**, a digital marketing studio focused on helping small coffee shops keep their website, listings, and local presence fresh.

## Business snapshot
- Name: Roast & Reach
- Niche: coffee shop marketing
- Public-facing goal: help independent coffee shops get more local visibility and better conversions
- Tone: modern, premium, simple, and practical

## Services / offers
- Website refreshes
- Local listing cleanup
- Search-friendly copy updates
- Conversion-focused landing pages
- Social / local presence improvements
