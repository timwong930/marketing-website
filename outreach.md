# Outreach

## Initial contact
- **Opener:** Hi, I’m Tim — I took a quick look at Roast & Reach and put together a simple idea for how the website could turn more visitors into calls or quote requests.
- **What to mention:**
  - Lead with outdated hours, menu links, and local listings.
  - Mention that small coffee shops lose calls and walk-ins when mobile info is stale.
- **Ask:** Offer a quick screenshot or 2-minute walkthrough — low pressure.
- **Tone:** Friendly, local, no-pressure, and short. Keep it practical and specific.
